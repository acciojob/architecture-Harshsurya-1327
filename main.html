Architecture Through the Ages

The Taj Mahal, built in the 17th century in India, is one of the most iconic examples of Mughal architecture. Its symmetrical design, marble domes, and intricate carvings symbolize the artistic achievements of that era.
The Sagrada Família in Barcelona, designed by Antoni Gaudí, represents modernist architecture at its peak. The cathedral’s unique curves, organic shapes, and symbolic details have made it a masterpiece still under construction.
